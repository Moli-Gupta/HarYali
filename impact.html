Climate Change Impacts

Climate change leads to rising temperatures, causing heatwaves and disrupting ecosystems. Melting ice caps and glaciers contribute to sea level rise, endangering coastal communities and habitats. Shifts in weather patterns increase the frequency of droughts and floods.

Human health is impacted by heat-related illnesses, spreading diseases, and deteriorating air quality. Economically, agricultural disruptions, damage to infrastructure, and increased insurance costs strain resources. Climate change exacerbates social inequalities and triggers displacement and migration.
